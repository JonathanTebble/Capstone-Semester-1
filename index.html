Frequently asked questions

Here's some of the questions everyone wants to know the answer to ...

What is an Epic Retirement?

An 'epic retirement' is a term coined by Bec Wilson, author of the bestselling book How to Have an Epic Retirement. It refers to living your retirement years with energy, purpose, and passion. It's about making the most of your longer, healthier life, enjoying your financial freedom, and filling your time with meaningful activities and experiences, rather than simply coasting through on a passive income. An epic retirement is about thriving, not just surviving.

What is the Epic Retirement Flagship Course?

The How to Have an Epic Retirement Flagship Course is a six week program run by Bec Wilson to help people work through the epic retirement lessons in a hands-on and guided manner. It's an online education program that comes with a stunning professionally printed workbook. The program is run 'synchronously' meaning it's done as a group cohort, and there is six live Q&A events held over the six week course.

Where can I buy a copy of How to Have an Epic Retirement - the book?

You can buy a printed copy via Amazon and you can find it in store in most Big W and QBD stores in Australia.

You can purchase an ebook on Amazon, or access an audio book on Audible or Spotify.

How often do you run the How to Have an Epic Retirement Flagship Course?

Bec Wilson runs this course four times a year for the public - one each season. Sign up for our expression of interest list to be notified of the next upcoming course dates.
